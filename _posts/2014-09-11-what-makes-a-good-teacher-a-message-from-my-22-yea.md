I had yet to teach a single lesson.</span></div><div style="text-align: left;"><span style="background-color: white; color: #363636; font-family: &quot;roboto slab&quot;, &quot;helvetica neue light&quot;, &quot;helvetica neue&quot;, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px;"><br /></span></div><div style="text-align: left;"><span style="background-color: white; color: #363636; font-family: &quot;roboto slab&quot;, &quot;helvetica neue light&quot;, &quot;helvetica neue&quot;, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px;">Despite being so wet behind the ears back then, I identified these two things that I thought made a good teacher:</span></div><div style="text-align: left;"><ol style="text-align: left;"><li>The best teachers are 'masters of their subject'. They lead by example; practice what they preach. Their academic record is one of excellence in their chosen field.</li><li>The best teachers are utterly committed to teaching in its broadest sense. For them, it is not something that they do in the absence of anything better coming along. It defines them. For these teachers, teaching is a 'way of life, not just a job'. They are prepared to 'go the extra mile to help those in their charge'. They are fully involved in extra-curricular activities even when this means long additional hours 'for little or no reward'.</li></ol></div><div class="post-content" data-type-cleanup="true" style="background-color: white; background-repeat: no-repeat; box-sizing: border-box; color: #363636; font-family: &quot;roboto slab&quot;, &quot;helvetica neue light&quot;, &quot;helvetica neue&quot;, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: left;"><div style="background-repeat: no-repeat; box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 7.5px 0px;">I'm a little longer in the tooth now but I reckon my youthful self, unencumbered by thousands of hours of experience, got it pretty much spot on.</div><div style="background-repeat: no-repeat; box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 7.5px 0px;"><br /></div><div style="background-repeat: no-repeat; box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 7.5px 0px; text-align: center;"><img src="https://web.archive.org/web/20180826080932im_/http://postachio-images.s3-website-us-east-1.amazonaws.com/w600_bebac4503aad6717544d6d65cda623ee.png" /></div><div style="background-repeat: no-repeat; box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 7.5px 0px; text-align: center;"><img src="https://web.archive.org/web/20180826080932im_/http://postachio-images.s3-website-us-east-1.amazonaws.com/w600_ebceb15b28515ec2833131951681a18c.png" /></div></div>
