As a young teacher, I remember thinking she was superb; reading through her posts reminds me of why.&nbsp;</p><p style="background-color: white; color: #2b3031; font-family: Times; font-size: 16px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; margin: 0px; min-height: 19px;"><br /></p><p style="background-color: white; color: #2b3031; font-family: Times; font-size: 16px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; margin: 0px;">I hope Bryanston continue to leave up her posts up on their website as an archive.
