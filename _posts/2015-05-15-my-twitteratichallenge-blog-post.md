So there you have it. I've shown you mine, so now you show me yours...

The way this works, by the way, taken straight from <a href="https://twitter.com/TeacherToolkit" shape="rect">@teachertoolkit's</a> original post <a href="http://teachertoolkit.me/2015/04/29/twitteratichallenge-by-teachertoolkit/" target="_blank">on the challenge</a> is as follows:

Rules:

There are only 3 rules.

<ol>
<li>
You cannot knowingly include someone you work with in real life.
</li>
<li>
You cannot list somebody that has already been named if you are already made aware of them being listed on <a href="https://twitter.com/search?f=realtime&amp;q=%23TwitteratiChallenge&amp;src=typd" shape="rect" target="_blank">#TwitteratiChallenge</a>
</li>
<li>
You will need to copy and paste the title of this blog post and (the Rules and What To Do) information into your own blog post.
</li>
</ol>

What To Do?

There are 5 to-dos you must use if you would like to nominate your own list of colleagues.
<ol>
<li>
Within 7 days of being nominated by somebody else, you need to identify colleagues that you rely regularly on for support and challenge. They have now been challenged and must act as participants of the <a href="https://twitter.com/search?f=realtime&amp;q=%23TwitteratiChallenge&amp;src=typd" shape="rect" target="_blank">#TwitteratiChallenge.</a>
</li>
<li>
If you’ve been nominated, you must write your own <a href="https://twitter.com/search?f=realtime&amp;q=%23TwitteratiChallenge&amp;src=typd" shape="rect" target="_blank">#TwitteratiChallenge</a> blog post within 7 days. If you do not have your own blog, try <a href="http://staffrm.io/stories" shape="rect" target="_blank">@StaffRm.</a>
</li>
<li>
The educator nominated, that means you reading this must either: a) record a video of themselves (using <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/gb/app/periscope/id972909677?mt=8" shape="rect" target="_blank">Periscope</a>?) in continuous footage and announce their acceptance of the challenge, followed by a pouring of your (chosen) drink over a glass of ice.
</li>
<li>
Then, the drink is to be lifted with a ‘cheers’ before the participant nominates their five other educators to participate in the challenge.
</li>
<li>
The educator that is now (newly) nominated, has 7 days to compose their own <a href="https://twitter.com/search?f=realtime&amp;q=%23TwitteratiChallenge&amp;src=typd" shape="rect" target="_blank">#TwitteratiChallenge </a>blog post and identify who their top-5 go-to educators are.
</li>
</ol>
